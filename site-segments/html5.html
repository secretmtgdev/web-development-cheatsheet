Seemantics

  • Give meaning to the content of a page
  • Useful for accessibility, ex: screen readers/contrasting colors/captions
  • For images always add alt text
  • Semantic tags
    • nav - list of links that user can use to navigate the site
    • header - top segment of a page
    • main - the meat & potatoes of the page
    • aside - content that is unrelated to the page
    • footer - footnote of the page
    • article - independent content segment that is still related to the page
    • section - defines a specific portion of the page
    • details - used for displaying general idea of content on the page, I use it as a table of contents!
    • figure - used for visual images
    • mark - used for highlighting content
